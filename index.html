Natural Environment

Embodying yoga in nature helps us to re-establish this union and connection with our environment. Practicing outside in nature, especially surrounded by trees or in a forest, can help you become more still, calmer, more present, and more aware. It heightens your senses and self-awareness.

Friendly Environment

A friendly environment in yoga offers numerous benefits, including reduced stress, improved concentration, enhanced learning, increased motivation, emotional well-being, physical safety, social connection, self-acceptance, positive feedback, a stronger mind-body connection, openness to exploration, and adaptability. Such an environment fosters a sense of belonging, support, and acceptance, enabling practitioners to experience a more fulfilling and enriching yoga practice that positively impacts their physical, mental, and emotional well-being.
